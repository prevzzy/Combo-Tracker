About
Combo Tracker is an app that gathers and presents data about combos made in THUG Pro and reTHAWed. Its purpose is to significantly simplify analyzing combos, and help players improve and achieve their score goals faster. It is also destined to serve as an individual highscores list, that allows the user to have all of the data of their best combos available at all times.
It's worth noting, that although the combo data is almost completely obtained from inside the game, either directly or by mimicking the game engine's logic, the application only keeps track of the values. At no point does it modify any of the in-game values in any way.
Glossary
Grind/Manual/Lip time
Total time spent on grinds, manuals or lips in a combo. The timer values are strictly correlated to how fast the player's balances are - the more time you spend on a particular balance type, the harder it is to control. These values are also increased or decreased based on applicable penalties and rewards listed below.
New grind
Any grind made on an object different from the one that the previous grind was started on. The game awards the player by subtracting around 0.086 seconds from the total time spent on grinds, making the grind balance a bit slower. The grind indicator moves back to the middle of the balance bar a bit with every new grind.
Double grind
Two consecutive grinds made on the same object. The game penalizes the player by adding 2 seconds to the grind timer value, making grind balance noticeably faster. When regrinding an object quickly, the grind balance indicator will also drastically change its position.
Tag limit
Exclusive to THUG, THUG2 and THUG Pro. Also known as robot line detection. A mechanism with the purpose of penalizing players who use very short lines to get high scores. In a single combo, one object can be grinded exactly 10 times without any penalty. The 11th grind (and every further one) on that object will make the grind balance significantly harder to control. The grind balance indicator will also become unpredictable and appear in different places than it normally should. 2 seconds are added to the grind timer value for the 11th grind, about 3.3 seconds for the 12th grind and 5 seconds for the 13th and every following grind.
Cheese penalty
Three or more consecutive manuals without a grind, revert, sticker slap/wallplant or a lip trick in-between. Every time this condition is met, 2 seconds are added to the manual timer value. The penalty doesn't apply to special manuals.
Pivot penalty
Whenever you do a pivot during a manual, 1 second is added to the manual timer value.
Revert speed penalty
Every revert in a combo adds a temporary speed penalty that lasts for 20 seconds and is applied to the next revert. If you do more than one revert in less than 20 seconds, then the penalties combine and result in a bigger speed loss.
For example, if a player does 3 consecutive reverts in a very short amount of time, it would take roughly 60 seconds of holding the combo without doing reverts for the penalty to disappear completely. The penalty slows the player down only when they do a revert - their general speed isn't affected in any way.
This mechanic also applies to landing pivots.
Trick usage
Trick score values decrease depending on how many times they have been used in a combo. The points awarded for a particular trick decrease by 25% of its initial value for the first 3 times it's used. After the 4th use, the value is finally decreased by 15%, making 5th and every following use of the trick give only 10% of its initial value. The trick percentage values can be presented as 100-75-50-25-10-10â€¦
Every trick will award the player a maximum of 9.5 multiplier - 1 for the first 9 times it's used, and a 0.5 for the 10th time it's used. After you reach that limit, the trick won't add any more multiplier.
These rules don't apply to gaps, which always award the player the same amount of base points and 1 multiplier.
The regular and switch variations of the same trick are distinguished and counted as different tricks - e.g. Kickflip and Switch Kickflip.
